Detailed Description

Read-write lock with policy to prefer writers.

Policy yamc::rwlock::WriterPrefer: Writer prefer locking. While any reader thread owns shared lock and there are a waiting writer thread, subsequent other reader threads which try to acquire shared lock are blocked until writer thread's work is done. This policy might introduce "Reader Starvation" if writer threads continuously try to acquire exclusive lock.
